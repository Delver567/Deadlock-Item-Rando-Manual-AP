Deadlock-Item-Rando-Manual-AP

The 3rd-Person Shooter MOBA of our dreams that still isn't out of sort-of closed alpha, Deadlock, now has a item rando Manual AP (poptracker included)!

The goal of this AP was to provide an item randomization experience in Deadlock, and a victory condition more likely to be completed in less time:
- Victory doesn't require winning a certain number of games due to game length, instead those wins are delegated by character categories and are normal checks.
- It also aims to give a more varied gameplay experience, with potential to focus on different characters depending on seed and other players.


HIGHLY RECOMMENDED TO NOT RANDOMIZE ALL ITEMS FOR BETTER EXPERIENCE
Recommended order for not randomizing items: Tier 4, Tier 2, Tier 3, Tier 1

Optional to randomize Street Brawl, consider the following when deciding on randomizing it:
- Faster games (on average)
- Items are luck dependent
  - May lock in items not unlocked
  - May not always find check items
- Many gameplay checks focus on Standard mode


Victory: Play a game AS/WITH/AGAINST an unlocked Character to collect a check and their Wish, and collect them all for the Patron to grant your wish and goal!
Goal Item Requirements: Unlocking all Characters


Starting Items: 3 random Characters
Items: All purchasable Items (not legendary), Street Brawl gamemode, all Characters and their Wishes
Filler Item Name: Soul


Locations:
 - Gameplay: Destroying every type of tower, delivering the soul urn, Team claiming the rejuvenator, collecting increments of
             souls, kills, damage, and healing, winning in every mode, and getting a legendary item (only in Street Brawl)

   - Rankings: Getting MVP, getting Key Player, and getting the most souls/kills/assists/plyr dmg/obj dmg/healing of all players
   - Stat Totals: Collecting increments of Souls (20k/200k), Kills/Assists (10/100), Damage (20k/200k), and Healing (5k/50k)

 - Items: Buying a randomized item

 - Characters: Playing AS/WITH/AGAINST unlocked characters and collecting their wishes from it, and winning games with characters
               sorted by their wishes and reasons for fighting (Power, Resolution, Normalcy, Answers, True Good, and Mystery)


Extra Rules:
 - Map Checks: If a check doesn't say "Team", you must be significantly involved in, OR present at the finish or fight leading to a check
               If a check does say "Team", anyone on your Team may do it without your involvement and the check will count

 - "Buying" Items: An item counts as bought if it is unlocked, obtained, and is not sold at full price

 - Healing: Healing is defined in this AP as Healing + Barrier Absorb. Self Damage is nullified bc of characters that focus on it (Geist, Victor)
 - Damage: Damage for the Stat Total check is defined in this AP as Player Damage + Object Damage.

 - Bots: All bot games must either be the "Hard" difficulty, or the "Online Co-op" standard mode. Bots count towards your character checks,
         but you must play at least "Online Co-Op" to achieve the MVP/Key Player/Most Stats checks (don't throw games you don't have to!)

 - Street Brawl: You must avoid items you don't have as much as possible. If an item you don't have is forced, you can play with that item,
                 but it will not count towards your item totals (any other penalty is up to user).
                 Additionally, all Street Brawl games will count as collecting 20k souls for check progression.
                 Street Brawl towers will also count towards map checks, but you must keep track of which lane you're in to see which tower to check.

 - Early Difficulty Spike: Due to the nature of the AP locking away accessible gameplay, getting maximized gameplay checks may prove exceedingly or impossibly 
                           difficult, locking players out early either until more items are unlocked, or, in the worst case, until that progression is somehow
                           achieved anyways. In this case, you may bypass the "in game with other players" requirements for those checks. The same goes for
                           playing with bots and counting them for the "winning games in x gamemode" checks.
