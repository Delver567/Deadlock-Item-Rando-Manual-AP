How the characters are sorted into categories: I wanted this to be in line with the
theming and lore of the game. Some of these (Wraith/McGinnis especially) are highly debatable,
but that's why the explanation is here. Characters are sorted alphabetically.

Power - The Selfish few who prioritize themselves and their goals above
all else, aiming to take a seat above or with authority, or aiming to
simply gain power to achieve their own goals.

- Apollo, Graves, Mina, Paradox, Seven, Vindicta, Yamato

Resolution - Those whose lives led them to a problem that must be solved,
but their lives (or deaths) shall never be the same.

- Grey Talon, Holliday, Lady Geist, Pocket, Sinclair

Normalcy - Those who wish to return to what once was, or for those who
want their own inner peace fulfilled in a different kind of life.

- Calico, Dynamo, Mo & Krill, Rem, Shiv, Silver, Vyper, Wraith

Answers - Those whose paths were shrouded in mystery from the beginning,
questions must be answered before the path can be further tread.

- Abrams, Kelvin, Paige, Victor

True Good - The Selfless who prioritize others before themselves, no matter
how their help may actually affect the world around them.

- Bebop, Celeste, Infernus, Ivy, Mirage, Viscous, Warden

Mystery - Those whose wish isn't clear, or has yet to be formed. However,
the Patrons may be able to convince them to join their cause...

- Billy, Doorman, Drifter, Haze, Lash, McGinnis, Venator
